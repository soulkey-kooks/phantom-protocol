Region 1: Trailbreak (Detaching from Default Systems)
Theme: Identifying and severing dependency roots

Map of Ties – List your digital and physical dependencies by category. (XP: 1,000)
Default Denial – Replace a high-risk default account or number with a privacy-respecting alternative. (XP: 1,250)
Signal Split – Create an isolated communication system (burner phone, SIM, or compartmentalized account). (XP: 1,500)
Utility Unlink – Research and outline how to live in a no-utility dwelling, and plan actionable next steps. (XP: 1,750)
Tetherless Mode – Spend a full day without touching any ID-linked service or network. (XP: 2,000)

Region 2: Paperfield (Bureaucracy and Legal Presence)
Theme: Understanding and controlling your paper trace

Document Drift – List and rank all current government-tied documents. (XP: 1,000)
Mail Mirage – Set up a mail forwarding, drop address, or holding service legally. (XP: 1,250)
Jurisdiction Walk – Research legal ID, domicile, and reporting shifts across borders or states. (XP: 1,500)
Parallel Filing – Build and document a dual-presence setup with minimal overlap. (XP: 1,750)
The Filing Ghost – Draft a lawful, plausible legal disappearance strategy with contingencies. (XP: 2,000)

Region 3: Quiet Path (Travel Without Trace)
Theme: Movement across terrain without data shadows

Grid Exit – Map how travel data is collected via transport systems in your area. (XP: 1,000)
Ticket of Fog – Acquire transport access (bus, metro, etc.) using cash or anonymized payment. (XP: 1,250)
Cross without Code – Travel a local boundary without signal emission, ID, or digital trail. (XP: 1,500)
Trail Mask – Practice route cloaking using variable paths and irregular movement patterns. (XP: 1,750)
The Phantom Trip – Complete a full local travel loop without leaving any digital trace. (XP: 2,000)

Region 4: Burnshell (Disposable Systems)
Theme: Creation and use of devices built to be discarded

Starter Flame – Set up a new burner phone, USB OS, or laptop from scratch. (XP: 1,000)
Compartment Shell – Operate the burner system for a full day using strict OPSEC. (XP: 1,250)
Timebomb Kernel – Configure scheduled self-deletion of files, logs, or history. (XP: 1,500)
Hardware Disavow – Learn and practice safe disposal or permanent repurposing of hardware. (XP: 1,750)
The Burn Off – Create and perform a symbolic or real ritual of digital/device death. (XP: 2,000)

Region 5: Voice Without Echo (Offline Comms)
Theme: Communication channels without persistent signal

Dead Drop Line – Create a paper or USB-based dead drop exchange with a partner. (XP: 1,000)
Mesh Signal – Use Bluetooth mesh or offline messaging tools for local transmission. (XP: 1,250)
Whisper Packet – Connect two devices using WiFi Direct or equivalent offline protocol. (XP: 1,500)
Paper Protocol – Design and test a cipher-based physical communication system. (XP: 1,750)
The Silence Code – Build and operate a small, temporary offline network for a trusted group. (XP: 2,000)

Region 6: Skintrace (Visual and Behavioral Recognition)
Theme: Disrupting surveillance through appearance and patterns

Face Null – Apply low-visibility appearance techniques: posture, gait, concealment. (XP: 1,000)
Pattern Break – Deliberately disrupt movement routines or commute patterns for a week. (XP: 1,250)
Camouflage Pulse – Build and test soft disguises or visual misdirection gear. (XP: 1,500)
Signature Smear – Identify and alter recognizable habits, clothes, or accessories. (XP: 1,750)
The Mirror Test – Attempt to pass unnoticed by someone familiar with you in a casual encounter. (XP: 2,000)

Region 7: Cash Field (Unlinked Finance)
Theme: Cash strategies, gift economies, and analog exchange

The Withdrawn – Live using only cash or non-traceable exchange for 48 hours. (XP: 1,000)
Relay Ritual – Conduct a multi-hop cash transfer via intermediaries or steps. (XP: 1,250)
The Gift Chain – Complete three exchanges of value without record or expectation. (XP: 1,500)
Hard Currency – Use or trade with analog alternatives (e.g., metals, tokens, goods). (XP: 1,750)
The Flowless Ledger – Operate for one week without generating a single digital financial record. (XP: 2,000)

Region 8: Nomad Logic (Portable Existence)
Theme: Living with minimal anchors and high adaptability

Pack the Frame – Assemble a complete mobile ops kit with daily function essentials. (XP: 1,000)
Anchors Map – Identify geographic, legal, and emotional anchors; reduce at least one. (XP: 1,250)
One Bag Trial – Operate entirely from a single bag for 72 hours, outdoors or in motion. (XP: 1,500)
Safe Stop Grid – Map legal, physical, and cost-based options for off-grid shelter. (XP: 1,750)
Fieldform – Enact a 3-day controlled relocation or simulate a full micro-migration. (XP: 2,000)

Region 9: Veillines (Social Cloaking)
Theme: Managing social presence without detection

Presence Audit – List all public and private social presence touchpoints. (XP: 1,000)
Curtain Call – Begin soft withdrawal from one digital or physical social arena. (XP: 1,250)
Alternate Persona – Operate within a social context under a bounded pseudonymous identity. (XP: 1,500)
Exit Ritual – Remove yourself symbolically or actually from a group or platform. (XP: 1,750)
The Ghost Friend – Maintain a deep personal connection using zero traceable channels. (XP: 2,000)

Region 10: Nullgrid (Infrastructure Independence)
Theme: Surviving without public utilities or corporate services

Grid Sketch – Identify and document every utility or corporate system you rely on. (XP: 1,000)
Water Sigil – Secure legal access to a backup or non-utility water source. (XP: 1,250)
Power Ritual – Build or obtain a mobile off-grid power system. (XP: 1,500)
Signal Null – Operate for 24 hours with no internet, mobile, or GPS signal. (XP: 1,750)

Region 11: Bureau Ghost (Paper and Admin Obfuscation)
